About Us: Keeping Critical Operations Moving

Global Valves & Bearings Solutions is a premier supplier of industrial mechanical components dedicated to supporting the mining, general industrial, and oil & gas sectors.

Our Mission

To supply reliable and high-quality industrial components that ensure operational continuity, safety, and efficiency through trusted products, technical support, and committed service.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a trusted global supplier known for reliability, responsiveness, and long-term partnerships within critical industries.

We understand that in demanding and continuous operating environments, there is no room for error. Our focus is on delivering materials designed to ensure equipment reliability and reduced downtime. From the crushing plant to the conveyor line, we act as a partner in your supply chain.
